Karacter — learn Chinese simply

An offline Chinese helper built by a student learner: search words & expressions, draw characters by hand, hear native pronunciations, save vocabulary, and study — no internet or VPN required.

Download on the App Store Follow on Instagram
  • Offline-first — full dictionary & audio without connection

What Karacter does

Designed for learners — simple, reliable, and private.

Fast search

Search Chinese characters, pinyin, English translations, and full expressions instantly.

Draw characters

Hand-draw any character to look it up — perfect when you don’t know the spelling.

Pronunciations & review

Native audio for tones and pronunciations + simple saving for future study sessions.

Private & local

Karacter does not collect personal data — everything essential works locally on your device.

Screenshots

Real screens from the app — tap or click to enlarge (replace these images in `images/`).

AI helpers (optional)

If you want deeper explanations for a sentence, Karacter can break it down into understandable blocks and explain grammar and nuance.

  • Sentence analysis — breakdown and translation tips.
  • Study suggestions — spaced repetition-friendly saving and review.
  • More languages — planned: French, Spanish, Chinese UI & more.
